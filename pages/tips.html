Travel Tips for Bali

Plan your trip with confidence and ease.

Currency

The local currency is the Indonesian Rupiah (IDR).

Transport

Taxis and ride-hailing apps are common in urban areas.

Safety

Bali is generally safe, but always be mindful of your belongings.

Best Time to Visit

The dry season (April to October) is ideal for beach activities and sightseeing.
